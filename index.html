About Me

Hello, world, I', Isse Ibrahim, Web Designer, Developer, illustrator, photographer, graphic and motion designer based mostly in Kiev Ukraine.

Since childhood, I have been good at drawing and have taken a great interest in design. I like to think of design as a way to show users the hidden benefits of a product. My goal is to bring complicated tasks to simple meanings.


I work in Figma, Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and many other programs that help me create banners, graphic and web designs, illustrations for books, print advertising materials, and so on.
